Why i study coding?

It's a complicated question but let's take a closer look

I wouldn't say that coding was my dream or it's something i wanted to do as a kid. It all started when i was twelve. It's just happened to be covid time and software developing got really popular. I was watching videos online, getting to know something about programming languages. Then i tried to code myself. Watched more videos, looked for "programming" memes. Maybe that made me feel proud, to understand all of those fancy words. Some kid's mere interest.

Then i got to know people who was interested by it too. I attended some competition for girls, who tried to solve problems with digital solutions, like apps. I saw them and worked wih them and probably something clicked in me. That was the community i saw myself in. There were the people i wanted to look up to, to be like.

Right now my goal is to go to college in "computer science" major to be with people like this, to build something together and improve every day. After what i could work on indepentent project or work at company with a team and solve problems together. Coding is a way to connect, to build and create to me.
